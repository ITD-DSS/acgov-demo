Inclusive Child Care

Created by the Committee on Special Needs, this document answers 8 common questions regarding inclusive child care for all child care providers in Alameda County. This document also includes a very useful list of resources for support or technical assistance regarding caring for children with special needs.

These documents are provided in PDF (Portable Document Format). You will need the free Adobe Reader to open them.
