Angie has been the Program Administrator for Alameda County's Early Care and Education Program since 2000. LaWanda Wesley
Planning Council Coordinator and Quality Improvement Program Manager
1401 Lakeside Dr., 11th Fl. Oakland, CA 94612
Phone: 510-208-9651 Fax: 510-208-9579

LaWanda Wesley joined Alameda County June 2015 after spending 10 years in the field of early care and education in providing training and technical assistance to northern California early childhood programs. Additionally, she has a long-standing history as a collaborator on state and county early childhood initiatives. She now serves as Alameda County's Planning Council Coordinator and Quality Improvement Program Manager. Child Care Exchange, a national leadership organization, has recognized Dr. Wesley as one of the emerging leaders in the country. As an Oakland, California native with humble beginnings, she is dedicated to changing the lives of all children, especially those coming from communities of poverty and furthest reach from opportunity. She has a BA from CSU Chico in Child Development, an MA from CSU, Sacramento in Educational Leadership and Management, and an Ed.D in Educational Leadership and Management from Drexel University.
