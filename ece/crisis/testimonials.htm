"With a daughter of my own, I often struggle to find affordable child care. The irony, of course, is that I am an early educator myself. But without earning more, I have very few options. That's why I'm a part of the movement to fight for $15 and affordable child care for all, so that the next generation won't have to fight the same battles all over again."

- Eboni Warren, Former Staff, YMCA of the East Bay

"I am encouraged and excited to work in early education. Working with children is a really special thing to do, but I worry for the future because I don't know whether I am going to be able to live in a stable environment and earn a decent living if I stay in this field."

- Dalia Duran, early childhood development student at Las Positas College
